# Travel & Expense Policy

## Booking

All flights, hotels, and rental cars must be booked through Navan (formerly TripActions).

## Flight class

- Domestic flights: economy class regardless of duration.
- International flights under 6 hours: economy class.
- International flights 6+ hours: premium economy permitted.
- VP-level and above: business class for flights 6+ hours.

## Per diem (meals)

- Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo): $90/day
- Tier 2 cities (most other major cities): $70/day
- Tier 3 (everywhere else): $55/day

Receipts required for individual meals over $40.

## Reimbursable

- Ground transport to/from airport
- Wi-Fi on flights over 3 hours
- Checked baggage (one bag per trip)
- Laundry on trips longer than 5 days

## Not reimbursable

- Mini-bar
- In-room movies
- Personal entertainment
- Companion travel
