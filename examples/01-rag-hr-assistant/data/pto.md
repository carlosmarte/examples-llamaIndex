# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Annual accrual

- 0–2 years tenure: 15 days/year
- 3–5 years: 20 days/year
- 6+ years: 25 days/year

PTO accrues monthly (1/12 of the annual rate per pay period).

## Carryover

Up to 5 days of unused PTO carry over to the next calendar year. Excess balances are forfeited on January 1.

## Sick leave

Separate from PTO. 10 days/year, no carryover. No doctor's note required for absences under 3 consecutive days.

## Bereavement

Up to 5 paid days for immediate family, 2 paid days for extended family.

## Holidays

11 paid company holidays per year. See the internal calendar for current-year dates.
