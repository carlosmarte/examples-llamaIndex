# Parental Leave Policy

Effective 2024-01-01. Applies to all full-time employees of Acme Corp.

## Eligibility by tenure

| Tenure at expected leave start | Paid leave | Unpaid leave (job-protected) |
|---|---|---|
| Under 12 months | 0 weeks | 12 weeks (FMLA only, where applicable) |
| 12–23 months | 8 weeks | 4 weeks |
| 24 months or more | 16 weeks | 4 weeks |
| 60 months or more | 20 weeks | 4 weeks |

"Tenure" is measured from continuous-service start date.

## How to request

1. Notify your manager and HR Business Partner at least 60 days before the expected leave start.
2. Submit the Parental Leave Request form in Workday.
3. Coordinate handoff plan with your manager.

## Benefits during leave

- Health insurance: continues at active-employee contribution rate.
- 401(k): contributions pause; vesting continues.
- Equity: vesting continues uninterrupted.

## Return to work

Phased return is available — 50% schedule for the first 4 weeks back, at full pay.
Contact HR if you need accommodations.
