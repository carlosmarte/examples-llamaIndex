# Acme Corp — Q3 2025 Quarterly Report (excerpt)

## Revenue

Total revenue for the quarter ended September 30, 2025 was **$48.2 million**, an increase of 14% year-over-year from $42.3 million in Q3 2024.

Segment breakdown:

| Segment | Q3 2025 | Q3 2024 | YoY |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Networking hardware | $31.4M | $28.9M | +8.7% |
| SaaS subscriptions  | $14.1M | $10.4M | +35.6% |
| Professional services | $2.7M | $3.0M | -10.0% |

## Geography

- Americas: $29.8M (62%)
- EMEA: $12.4M (26%)
- APAC: $6.0M (12%)

## Outlook

Management projects Q4 2025 revenue in the range of $51M–$54M.
