# Acme Networking — Warranty Policy

## Coverage period

All Router series devices (X100, X200, X500) carry a **24-month limited warranty** from date of purchase.

Proof of purchase (receipt or order email) is required for any RMA.

## What's covered

- Manufacturing defects
- Component failure under normal use (caps, flash, ethernet PHYs)
- DOA units (report within 14 days)

## What's not covered

- Physical damage (drops, liquid, lightning)
- Tampered seals
- Devices flashed with non-official firmware
- Cosmetic wear

## RMA process

1. Open a support ticket with the device serial number and a description of the issue.
2. Support will troubleshoot first; if the device is confirmed defective and in-warranty, a replacement unit ships within 3 business days.
3. Return the original unit using the prepaid label within 14 days of receiving the replacement.
